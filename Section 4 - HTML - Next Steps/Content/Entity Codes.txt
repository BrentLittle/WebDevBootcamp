What is &#9824; ?

an entity code is used to display reserved characters that would be invalid

Can be used in place of hard to type characters

The browser renders and displays the correct character

What characters are reserved?
    - < and >
        <p> 1 &lt; 6 &gt; 1 </p>
    
    - &
        <p> 1 &lt; 6 &gt; 1 &amp; 1 &le; 6 &ge; 1</p>
